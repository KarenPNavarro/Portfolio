The Weather Gaze App is a simple tool to keep up to date with the latest weather conditions on the go. The app offers a variety of features such as real time temperatures, weather conditions and seven day forecast.

Amor al Horno

Amor al Horno is a vegan bakery located in Puerto Rico offering veganized alternatives to the islands most iconic desserts. The power of plants is revolutionizing the way we connect with our meals, Amor al Horno is veganizing our favorite Puerto Rican desserts with all the flavors we love and without compromising animals wellbeing.
